Sourdough Pizza

Using A's Sourdough Bakery Pizza Crust

What You Need

  • 1 A's Sourdough Pizza Crust (850g)
  • Pizza sauce (store-bought or homemade — recipe below)
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded (as much as you like)
  • Toppings of your choice

Directions

  1. Preheat your oven to 380–400°F.
  2. Place the pizza crust on a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  3. Spread a generous layer of pizza sauce over the crust.
  4. Add shredded mozzarella (and any other cheese you love).
  5. Add your favorite toppings.
  6. Bake for 8–10 minutes, until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Slice and enjoy!
A's favorite: Just cheese — a generous layer of mozzarella — with a few slices of jalapeño, then top with a big mound of fresh arugula right out of the oven. Nothing else needed!

Homemade Pizza Sauce

Makes enough for 2–3 pizzas

  • 1 can (398ml) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Pinch of sugar
  1. Heat olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic, cook 30 seconds.
  3. Add tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, sugar.
  4. Simmer 15–20 min, stir occasionally.
  5. Cool before spreading.
